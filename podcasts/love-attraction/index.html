Interview with Anders Sandberg. bio:

Anders Sandberg was born in Sweden and is now James Martin Research Fellow at the Future of Humanity Institute at Oxford University.

He is best known for his work on "transhumanism" - the benefits, ethics and dangers of new and future technologies that could overcome human limitations and greatly enhance human beings, perhaps transforming them into "transhumans".

portrait: /assets/img/anders_sandberg-larger-300.jpg length: 298999 ---

If you drink a couple of beers, you’ll notice various changes to your outlook on life. You may, for example, find that a person you previously found unappealing has miraculously morphed into somebody considerably more attractive. Anders Sandberg has been reflecting on the neurobiology of love and attraction – he wonders whether, in the future, we might consider popping love pills.
