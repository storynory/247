Most of us think that loving romantic relationships are of value – they’re among the things that make life worth living. But many people don’t find a suitable partner. With advances in robotics and artificial intelligence, a new question has arisen. Could humans have loving relationships with robots? Sven Nyholm tells us what that might entail.

Sven has contributed a chapter to Robot Sex edited by John Danaher and Neil Mcarthur
